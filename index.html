Rendix Alextian

Meet Rendix Alextian, a talented graphic designer with a passion for creating beautiful designs using Adobe Illustrator. Rendix has been working in the industry since 2013, and he has gained a wealth of experience working at iDesign Segovia. He started his own freelance business in 2015, specializing in vector design.

Rendix's love for design began when he was studying Multimedia at one of the top schools in Indonesia. During his time there, he discovered his passion for graphic design and realized that he had a talent for it. After graduating, he began working as a junior designer at iDesign Segovia, where he learned the ins and outs of the industry.

Over the years, Rendix has worked on numerous projects, including designing logos, and branding materials for a wide range of clients. His ability to combine typography, color, and imagery has helped him create stunning designs that stand out in a crowded market.

In 2015, Rendix decided to start his own business, focusing on vector design. He saw a need for high-quality vector designs that were affordable and accessible to small business owners and entrepreneurs. He quickly built a reputation for himself as a reliable and talented designer, and his business grew rapidly.

Rendix's expertise in Adobe Illustrator is unparalleled. He has spent countless hours mastering the software and developing his own unique style, which is both clean and modern. His designs are always on-trend and visually appealing, making them perfect for clients looking to stand out from the crowd.

When he's not working on client projects, Rendix enjoys creating personal projects that allow her to explore her creativity and push the boundaries of design. He also enjoys sharing her knowledge with others and has taught design workshops and mentored up-and-coming designers.

Rendix's passion for design is infectious, and he continues to inspire others with her stunning work and dedication to her craft.
